CEO's narrative that shapes their brand and resonates with their audience

Personal Branding: CEO marketing involves building and promoting the personal brand of the company's chief executive officer.

Reputation Management: It focuses on managing the CEO's reputation, online presence, and public image.

Thought Leadership: CEOs share their expertise and insights in their industry to establish themselves as thought leaders.

Social Media Engagement: CEOs use platforms like LinkedIn and Twitter to connect with the audience, share knowledge, and humanize the brand.

Content Creation: They may create content such as articles, blog posts, or videos to share their perspectives and insights.

Public Relations: CEO marketing may involve working with public relations professionals to manage media interactions and coverage.

Company Culture: CEOs can highlight the company's values and culture to showcase authenticity.

Community Engagement: They often engage with the community and support causes that align with the company's values.

Crisis Management: CEO marketing plays a role in addressing and managing crises that may affect the CEO's or company's reputation.

Alignment with Company Goals: CEO marketing efforts should align with the overall goals and mission of the company.

CEO marketing is about leveraging the CEO's influence to positively impact the company's reputation, public perception, and business objectives.
